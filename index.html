Proof of holding system

Experience the rewarding benefits of our unique "Proof of Holding" solution, designed to incentivize and empower users who hold Clore coins.

Increased Rewards:
The more Clore coins you hold, the more bonus $clore you receive for leasing your equipment.

Hassle-Free Usage:
No need to transfer money to our wallet. Simply sign the transaction from your desktop wallet to participate.

No Blocking:
Your money is not blocked; you're free to withdraw it for exchange or sale at your convenience.

Service Discounts:
Clore coin holders enjoy up to a 50% discount on our commission for services.

Optional Use:
Participation in Proof of Holding is entirely optional, but it's a great way to maximize the benefits of owning Clore coins.

No Penalties:
No restrictions or penalties apply if you decide to withdraw money from Proof of Holding.
